How to Play

Objective: Complete more rectangles than your opponent!

  • Click between two dots to draw a line
  • When you complete a rectangle, you get a point and another turn
  • If you don't complete a rectangle, the turn switches to the computer
  • The game ends when all possible lines are drawn
  • The player with the most rectangles wins!

Strategy: Try to avoid giving your opponent easy rectangles while setting up your own!
